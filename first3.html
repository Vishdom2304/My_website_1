My Education !


So guys, In this blog I will be talking about my education and along with my phase of life during those times.

So lets start with my high school times :-

I completed my studies till class 10th from Sunbeam School, Jaunpur and I have to say this, What a school it was till class 10th, Excellent.
The atmosphere so competitive and people so friendly, all these things made me work harder to be on the top.
I remember working hard through out those year just to become overall topper of all the sections which I managed to do only in the last year i.e. 10th itself.
And I can surely say, Those were one of the best times of my life, thanks to the fun that I did with my friends.
Then comes the part of life full of hustle and struggle, lol.

I completed my 12th from RBVM sr. sec. school Jaunpur. This was also the time when I was preparing for, "JEE". Surely, tough time, it was.
Anyway, Worked hard as much as I could and tried to do everything efficiently, learnt a lot of things both related to study and life.
Then came the exams, gave them and unfortunately wasn't able to get any NIT, IIT, e.t.c.
But again fortunately, got admission in IET Lucknow, One of the premiere institutes of Uttar Pradesh.
And thus my college life, begun !
And Now, its time for my current phase of life.

So after all that of 12th, I got into college i.e. Institute of Engineering and Technology (IET), Lucknow . A nice institute with friendly peers and some supportive seniors, and an atomsphere full of healthy competition.
I sometimes feel myself to be very fortunate for getting into such institute. Currently, I am in the 2nd year of Electronics and communication Engineering (ECE).
This phase of my life is all about working hard to improve my skills, Learning continiously and improving my personality too.

And Ya, one more thing, i.e., looking forward to get some good internships. So please recruiters, hire me !!!
Stay happy and Blessed, Take care !!!
